How it Works


With today's technology, it is super easy to be hired by companies and then do the job from the comfort of your own home with your family. At Tech-Ployment we'll help you explore online job opportunities and earn a living. Here are the 5 easy steps in applying your dream job!

STEP 1

Once you have an account, click the job board button

STEP 2

On the side panel, you can customize the type of job that you are looking

STEP 3

If you found the perfect job, click apply now!

STEP 4

Supply the require informations such as your contact number, resume or email

STEP 5

Wait for the reply of the employer and be ready to be hired!
