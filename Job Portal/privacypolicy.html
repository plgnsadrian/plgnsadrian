Privacy Policy

Tech-Ployment (henceforth "Tech-Ployment"/"we") recognizes the value of privacy. Tech-Ployement provides this Privacy Policy to inform users of the Tech-Ployment website the related services we provide ("Services"), and the Site about our policies and procedures or collecting, using, and disclosing information. Thank you for entrusting Tech-Ployment with your personal information. We feel that you should be aware of how we manage your personal information. We only collect information that you voluntarily give us, and we process it with your consent or another legal basis; we only need the bare minimum of personal information to complete the purpose of your interaction with us; we never sell it; and we only use it for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

1. What information Tech-Ployment collect Personal Information - Visitors to Tech-Ployment provide us with basic information as well as certain personally identifiable information. We only require the bare minimum of personal data from you. This section provides details. "User Personal Information" refers to any personal information about one of our users that may be used to personally identify them on its own or in conjunction with other data. A username and password, an email address, a real name, and a photograph are examples of "User Personal Information." “Technical information” includes information collected from internet browsers, such as web server logs, and other log information, such as User session or activity logs. Technical information can be linked to User Personal Information, such as a username or email address, as well as other potentially personally-identifying information, such as IP addresses. User Personal Information does not contain aggregated, non-personally identifying data. We may use aggregated, non-personally identifiable data to operate, analyze, improve, and optimize our website and services.
Non-Personal Information- We do not collect non-personal information such as the device you are using when visiting our website, the model of your devices, access to your own files and your connection to other website you are visiting.

2. How we use the information we collect- In order to provide the service you requested, we may share information with our vendors or with you. We do not sell your personal information or host advertisements on Tech-Ployment. We do not share, sell, rent, or swap User Personal Information with other parties for their commercial purposes unless you have specifically requested us to do so (such as when you apply for a job online). We do not allow advertising on Tech-Ployment. On its individual pages, Tech- Ployment does not fund or track any advertisements.

3. How you can access the information we collect- We provide ways to access, alter, delete the data you have provided in your personal information. You can also contact the support email concern.techploymentph@gmail.com for help.
