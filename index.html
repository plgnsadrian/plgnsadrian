A blog


It took me a lot of time deciding a title for this simple blog, and ta-dah, "A Blog." I never thought I would write a thing like this, but since no one who knows me in real life will be able to read this, why not?

This blog is more about my perspective on life; it may answer the question, what is the meaning of life? Should we live for others? I believe that if we follow what other people want us to be, we can be happy because seeing people you admire smile is already the definition of happiness to me. It may look like I'm selfless, but believe me, I'm not; it's more about being content or more than content when you reach others' expectations. Thus, for me, the meaning of life is to live for others. Being able to satisfy the needs and wants of your loved one is what matters. If you practice this mindset, there won't be a day where you'll blame yourself for not doing your best because every damn day, you will only give your 'best.' Living only for ourselves is not worth it. Our family will always be there for us at the end of the road, so why shouldn't we prioritize them? People often say, "Love yourself," no, it should be "Give love, and you will receive it back." Let me give you an example; if you pat yourself on your head, is it better when others do it? What's more important, is it when you're proud of yourself or when others are proud of you? Is the food you eat tastier when you cook it yourself, or is it better when others do it?

That would be the end of this rant-- I mean blog. It may not be a great one, but it is what I genuinely feel. But what I do feel does not matter. Always believe what you think is right because it is your life. After days, weeks, or months, my perspective may change, and this is what scares me. I hope it will never change.
